The Couple

Vy and Matthew went to the same high school but did not know one another, he was a year older and Vy studied and read books. In college, they became part of the same circle of friends but only saw and talked to one another in group get togethers. Fast forward several years, Matthew calls Luis, Vy’s college best friend, to see if he could come over and hang out. It just so happened that Vy was already at Luis’ apartment. After that, Vy and Matthew became close friends until one day Matthew decided to go past the point of no return and started to show interest in Vy.

This past summer, on their last night of vacation at Harry Potter World, Matthew went down on one knee and asked Vy to be his wife. After telling Matthew to “shut up”, crying, and overcoming shock Vy finally said, “YES!!!”.
