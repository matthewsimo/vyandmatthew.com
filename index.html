The Couple

Vy and Matthew both grew up in Houston and even went to the same high school. They finally met in college, where they had an overlapping circle of friends. Still, they only saw one another briefly or in passing.

Matthew started to become closer friends with Luis, who was Vy's best friend in college. By becoming friends with Luis, Matthew got to know Vy as well. Their friendship grew until Matthew had no choice but to pursue Vy romantically.

While on vaction in Florida, Matthew bent down on one knee and asked Vy to be his wife. Vy was so stunned that her first words were "Shut up!", after gathering her composure she said, "YES!"
